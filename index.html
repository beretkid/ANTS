"What the hell is this???"
This is the menu for inputting your very own custom behaviors!
It works by simply running things inputted here as javascript--Don't worrry if you don't know any!
Its very simplified, and has even premade functions that are listed later below.

The X & Y Fields is what it ADDS every frame
The Optional V Field works as a custom variable like a direction, it is what it gets SET to every frame
Heres an list of all the functions and variables:

sin(value), Cos(value), and tan(value) -- self explanitory i hope
x, y, v -- I really hope you know what the first two are, as for V please move your eyeballs up a bit.
random(min,max) -- also hopefully self explanitory
mod(value,modulus) -- Modulo, basicly loops back to zero if value exeeds modulus.
t -- the age of the current ANT; goes up every frame, and goes well with sin and cos!

Remember that this IS running javascript, so you can actully just write most valid js like Math and sutch!
ALSO REALLY IMPORTANT NOTE!!! none of this is capitalized at all don't ever capitalize anything in the codes!
Baicly avoid caps lock like the plauge, and dont ever use any capitalized letters in the code.
Note: floats (numbers with decimals) are supported, but visualy will be rounded eg: 3.14 will look like 3, if that helps.
